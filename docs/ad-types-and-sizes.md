# Ad Types and Sizes — Nova Public Notices Portal

**Complete reference for all ad types, sizes, and placements**

---

## Standard Display Ads

### 1. Leaderboard
- **Size:** 728×90px
- **Placement:** Top of page, above content
- **Desktop only**
- **Best for:** Brand awareness, high visibility

### 2. Super Leaderboard
- **Size:** 970×90px
- **Placement:** Top of page, homepage hero
- **Desktop only**
- **Best for:** Premium brand campaigns

### 3. Billboard
- **Size:** 970×250px
- **Placement:** Homepage hero, category pages
- **Desktop only**
- **Best for:** High-impact brand campaigns

### 4. Half Page (Large Rectangle)
- **Size:** 300×600px
- **Placement:** Sidebar, right rail
- **Desktop only**
- **Best for:** Rich media, engagement

### 5. Medium Rectangle (MPU)
- **Size:** 300×250px
- **Placement:** Sidebar, in-content
- **Desktop + Tablet**
- **Best for:** Most versatile, high CTR

### 6. Large Rectangle
- **Size:** 336×280px
- **Placement:** Sidebar, in-content
- **Desktop + Tablet**
- **Best for:** Standard display

### 7. Wide Skyscraper
- **Size:** 160×600px
- **Placement:** Sidebar, right rail
- **Desktop only**
- **Best for:** Vertical content

### 8. Skyscraper
- **Size:** 120×600px
- **Placement:** Sidebar, right rail
- **Desktop only**
- **Best for:** Narrow sidebars

---

## Mobile Ads

### 9. Mobile Leaderboard
- **Size:** 320×50px
- **Placement:** Top/bottom of mobile screen
- **Mobile only**
- **Best for:** Mobile brand awareness

### 10. Large Mobile Banner
- **Size:** 320×100px
- **Placement:** Top/bottom of mobile screen
- **Mobile only**
- **Best for:** Enhanced mobile visibility

### 11. Mobile Rectangle
- **Size:** 300×250px
- **Placement:** In-content on mobile
- **Mobile + Tablet**
- **Best for:** In-feed ads

### 12. Mobile Full Width
- **Size:** 100% width × 90px (fluid)
- **Placement:** Between content sections
- **Mobile only**
- **Best for:** Responsive mobile ads

---

## Interstitial & Overlay Ads

### 13. Interstitial (Full Screen)
- **Size:** 100vw × 100vh (full screen)
- **Placement:** Between page transitions
- **All devices**
- **Best for:** High-impact campaigns
- **Frequency:** Max 1 per session
- **Dismissable:** Yes (close button)

### 14. Modal Overlay
- **Size:** 600×400px (desktop), 90vw×60vh (mobile)
- **Placement:** Center overlay
- **All devices**
- **Best for:** Promotions, newsletters
- **Dismissable:** Yes

### 15. Lightbox
- **Size:** Variable (expands on click)
- **Placement:** Expands from standard ad
- **Desktop + Tablet**
- **Best for:** Rich media, video

---

## Sticky & Fixed Ads

### 16. Sticky Footer (Anchor Ad)
- **Size:** 320×50px (mobile), 728×90px (desktop)
- **Placement:** Bottom of viewport (fixed)
- **All devices**
- **Best for:** Persistent visibility
- **Dismissable:** Yes

### 17. Sticky Sidebar
- **Size:** 300×250px or 300×600px
- **Placement:** Sidebar (scrolls then sticks)
- **Desktop only**
- **Best for:** Long-form content

### 18. Sticky Header
- **Size:** 728×90px (desktop), 320×50px (mobile)
- **Placement:** Top of viewport (fixed)
- **All devices**
- **Best for:** Maximum visibility
- **Note:** Can be intrusive, use sparingly

---

## Native & In-Feed Ads

### 19. In-Feed Native
- **Size:** Variable (matches content cards)
- **Placement:** Between content cards
- **All devices**
- **Best for:** High engagement, native feel

### 20. Recommended Content
- **Size:** 300×250px or card-sized
- **Placement:** Below article, related content
- **All devices**
- **Best for:** Content discovery

### 21. Sponsored Listing
- **Size:** Same as notice cards
- **Placement:** In search results, category pages
- **All devices**
- **Best for:** Performance marketing

---

## Video Ads

### 22. In-Stream Video
- **Size:** 640×360px (16:9), 640×480px (4:3)
- **Placement:** Video player
- **All devices**
- **Best for:** Pre-roll, mid-roll video

### 23. Out-Stream Video
- **Size:** Variable (in-content)
- **Placement:** Between paragraphs
- **All devices**
- **Best for:** Autoplay video ads

### 24. Video Slider
- **Size:** 300×250px (expands to video)
- **Placement:** Sidebar, in-content
- **Desktop + Tablet**
- **Best for:** Engaging video content

---

## Expandable & Rich Media

### 25. Expandable Banner
- **Size:** 728×90px → 728×300px (expands on hover)
- **Placement:** Top of page
- **Desktop only**
- **Best for:** Rich content reveal

### 26. Peel Ad (Page Curl)
- **Size:** Corner fold effect
- **Placement:** Top-right corner
- **Desktop only**
- **Best for:** Novelty, high engagement

### 27. Pushdown Ad
- **Size:** 970×90px → 970×415px (pushes content down)
- **Placement:** Top of page
- **Desktop only**
- **Best for:** Premium campaigns

## Takeover & Wallpaper

### 28. Wallpaper Ad (Background Takeover)
- **Size:** Full background (centered content)
- **Placement:** Page background
- **Desktop only**
- **Best for:** Brand immersion

### 29. Skin Ad (Left/Right Takeover)
- **Size:** 200px left + 200px right sidebars
- **Placement:** Page sides
- **Desktop only**
- **Best for:** High-impact branding

### 30. Homepage Takeover
- **Size:** Multiple placements
- **Placement:** All homepage ad slots
- **Desktop + Mobile**
- **Best for:** Exclusive brand campaigns

---

## Popup & Slide-In

### 31. Entry Popup (Welcome Gate)
- **Size:** 600×400px (desktop), 90vw×60vh (mobile)
- **Placement:** On page load
- **All devices**
- **Best for:** Email capture, announcements
- **Frequency:** Max 1 per 24 hours

### 32. Exit Intent Popup
- **Size:** 600×400px (desktop), 90vw×60vh (mobile)
- **Placement:** On exit intent
- **Desktop + Tablet**
- **Best for:** Retention, conversions
- **Mobile:** On scroll-up

### 33. Slide-In (Corner)
- **Size:** 400×300px
- **Placement:** Bottom-right corner
- **All devices**
- **Best for:** Non-intrusive notifications

### 34. Timed Popup
- **Size:** 600×400px (desktop), 90vw×60vh (mobile)
- **Placement:** After X seconds on page
- **All devices**
- **Best for:** Engagement campaigns

---

## Special & Custom

### 35. Floating Ad
- **Size:** 300×250px
- **Placement:** Floats over content
- **Desktop + Tablet**
- **Best for:** Attention-grabbing
- **Note:** Can be annoying, use carefully

### 36. Slider Ad
- **Size:** 300×250px (slides in from side)
- **Placement:** Side of viewport
- **Desktop only**
- **Best for:** Notifications, offers

### 37. Scroll-Triggered Ad
- **Size:** Variable
- **Placement:** Appears at X% scroll
- **All devices**
- **Best for:** Engagement at key moments

### 38. Time-Based Rotation
- **Size:** Any standard size
- **Placement:** Any standard placement
- **All devices**
- **Best for:** Multiple advertisers, A/B testing

## Recommended Placements by Page Type

### Homepage
- Super Leaderboard (970×90) — Above fold
- Billboard (970×250) — Hero area
- Medium Rectangle (300×250) — Sidebar
- Half Page (300×600) — Right rail
- In-Feed Native — Between sections
- Sticky Footer — Mobile

### Search Results / Category Archive
- Leaderboard (728×90) — Above results
- Medium Rectangle (300×250) — Sidebar
- In-Feed Native — Every 5-10 results
- Sponsored Listings — Top 3 positions
- Sticky Footer — Mobile

### Single Notice Page
- Leaderboard (728×90) — Above content
- Medium Rectangle (300×250) — Sidebar (sticky)
- In-Content Native — Mid-article
- Recommended Content — Below article
- Sticky Footer — Mobile

### Submit / Checkout Pages
- **NO ADS** — Per Guidelines.md

## Ad Frequency & User Experience Rules

### Maximum Ad Density
- **Desktop:** 30% of viewport
- **Mobile:** 20% of viewport
- **Never:** More than 1 interstitial per session

### Popup Frequency
- Entry popup: Max 1 per 24 hours
- Exit intent: Max 1 per session
- Timed popup: Max 1 per page view
- Total popups: Max 2 per session

### Dismissability
- All overlay ads must be dismissable
- Close button must be visible and accessible
- Sticky ads must have close option

### Loading
- Ads should not shift content (reserve space)
- Lazy load below-the-fold ads
- Never block content with ads

## Ad Creative Guidelines

### Image Specs
- Format: JPG, PNG, GIF, SVG
- Max file size: 150KB (standard), 500KB (rich media)
- Animation: Max 30 seconds, 5 loops

### Text
- Headline: Max 25 characters
- Body: Max 90 characters
- CTA: Max 15 characters

### Branding
- Logo required
- Brand colors
- Consistent messaging

### Accessibility
- Alt text for all images
- Sufficient color contrast
- Keyboard dismissable
